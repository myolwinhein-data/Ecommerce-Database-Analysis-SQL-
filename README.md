## Business Questions ( KPIs )
- Where do our customers live, and how is our gender distribution?
- Which payment methods are most popular, and which product categories drive the most revenue?
- Who are our "Whale" customers, and how do they rank against each other?

## Key Insights
- The customer base is globally diverse,with USA, Japan, and Singapore emerging as the top three regions.
- The Electronics and Books categories are high-performing in terms of volume and unit price accumulation.
- Using the CASE statement logic,identified a clear hierarchy.Customers are classified as Gold Members,Silver Members and Bronze Members representing the lifetime value.
- The use of LEAD and LAG functions revealed how individual customers fluctuate in their spending over time.

## Final Conclusion
- This analysis shows that the business can grow by keeping popular items in stock and using customer spending habits to create better rewards for its most loyal shoppers.By understanding these patterns, the company can make smarter decisions to increase sales and keep customers coming back.
